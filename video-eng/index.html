How it works
Our process — Scan → Builder → System → Factory → Install
1
RenoChain Scan. We quickly scan the building to extract measurements and volumes. A high-fidelity digital twin is created for engineering and costing.
2
RenoChain Builder. Our software imports the scan model and constructs a new, energy-efficient wall system. A complete, itemized quote is typically ready in ~30 minutes.
3
RenoChain System. The building system is engineered to be cost-effective, sustainable, and climate-smart with standardized interfaces and verified performance.
4
RenoChain Factory. A smart-factory approach balances automation with skilled craftsmanship to deliver consistent quality, fair production, and reliable lead times.
5
RenoChain Install. Professional installation teams complete a full envelope install on an average-sized property in about three weeks, with standardized QA and documentation.
